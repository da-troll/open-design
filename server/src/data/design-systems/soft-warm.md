# Soft Warm Design System

## Brand
Approachable, cosy, human-centred products. Think Notion, Loom, Beehiiv. Warm neutrals, personal.

## Colors
- Background: #FAFAF9 (warm off-white)
- Surface: #FFFFFF
- Surface 2: #F3F2EF
- Border: #E4E3DF
- Text primary: #1A1916
- Text secondary: #706F6A
- Accent: #D97757 (warm terracotta)
- Accent light: #F2C4A8
- Success: #3D8F6C
- Info: #4A7FA5

## Typography
- Font: "Geist", "Plus Jakarta Sans", "DM Sans", system-ui, sans-serif
- Heading: 600 weight, warm tracking
- Body: 400 weight, 15–16px, 1.65 line-height
- UI label: 500 weight, 13px

## Spacing
- Base unit: 4px
- Relaxed rhythm: 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 40
- Border radius: 8px standard, 12px cards, 4px small elements

## Component Patterns
- Buttons: warm fill colors, soft radius (8px), gentle shadow
- Cards: white background, soft border, 12px radius
- Empty states: illustrated or emoji-forward
- Input: rounded, soft border, focus in accent warm color
- Dividers: dashed or dotted for informality

## Voice
Friendly. Conversational. First-person where appropriate. Emoji OK sparingly.
